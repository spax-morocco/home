What is SPAx?

SPAx, which stands for Student Professional Awareness Programs, is a program that started in 1976. In the over 40 years since its inception, thousands of events have taken place around the world. These activities are organized by IEEE Student Branches and offer students the opportunity to listen to experienced engineers from diverse backgrounds discuss topics spanning various professional development categories. The "x" in SPAx is a variable. It could denote a conference featuring an invited speaker, a series of activities aimed at honing career skills, a social event designed to foster networking and team-building skills, a design contest, a tech talk, or any other format, provided that the events focus on career development.

SPAx ENSAF SB :

Marks the first of its kind in Morocco, a pioneering event dedicated to fostering innovation and collaboration. Hosted in the vibrant city of Fes, this one-day event on May 2nd, 2024, brings together the realms of technology, engineering, healthcare, and energy for an unparalleled gathering of ideas and solutions. Featuring engaging tech talks, and opportunities for networking, it promises to be a hub of inspiration for those at the forefront of innovation. This event is all about sharing knowledge, exploring new trends, and collaborating on sustainable solutions to shape a better future.
